Healing and Wellness

The concept of Healing and Wellness is broad in that it encompasses many aspects of building a quality of life for Indigenous peoples. Hare Consulting Group focuses on Homelessness, Addictions and Mental Health, Elder and Community relationships and Indigenous Incarceration rehabilitation and prevention. Indigenous spiritual services will be an integral part of the healing process where an Elder will be engaged to perform talking and healing circles.
